    Panda Friends

    <Panda Friends> is the second panda art collection by artist Grey (Panda by Grey) currently based out of Seoul, Republic of Korea.

    The year 2021, three panda friends, Mei Lan, Qi Yi, and Cheng Shi, are turned 5 years old.

    This art collection contains the flow of stories of the three pandas' happy childhood, breaking up for a while and missing each other, and meeting again on their five-year-old birthday. It is made of three sets of "Pumpkin Princess, Mei Lan", "Wi-Fi Princess, Qi Yi", and "Little Grey, Cheng Shi" and each containing 13 artworks. It consists of artworks with special story from when they were babies to their 5th birthday. I'd like to give fun and create an impression as visual elements that reflect their individual personalities and beautiful images.

    These three pandas are some of my favorite pandas, and they provided me with motivation to start my panda artwork and a lot of inspiration. I planned this project because I would like to share the warm, friendly, and happy feelings I felt from them to many people.
    Especially, I reflected their identity by expressing each panda's lovely characters realistically. They are so precious just the way they are, I certainly think these artistic records are very meaningful not only panda fans but also our world.

    Through my artworks, I hope that the loveliness of the pandas, the preciousness of the friendship, and the happy and warm feelings will be conveyed to many people.

    Pumpkin Princess, Mei Lan

    This artwork is based on pumpkin princess Mei Lan, who likes pumpkin very much, and beautifully expresses her precious friendship with her childhood friends Qi Yi and Cheng Shi.

    The warm and bright orange color, which symbolizes pumpkin princess, is the main color and captures the vitality and sensibility through a soft and fantastic pastel tone color combination.

    This artwork set consists of a total of 13 artworks and includes additionally cover, prologue, and story pages (16 prints in total).
